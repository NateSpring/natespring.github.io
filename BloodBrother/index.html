BloodBrother

Glucose-based food/drink recommendations.
*This is a proof of concept app, contact a Doctor or nutritionist for recommendations on diet.*

Current Blood Glucose: mg/dL

Glucose Legend:

  • Less than 50 prompts warning alert.
  • Higher than 50, less than 100 returns carbs > 50 grams.
  • Higher than 100, less than 150 returns carbs < 35 grams.
  • Higher than 150, less than 200 returns carbs < 5 grams.
  • Higher than 200 prompts warning alert.
